A living room turned photo booth.

A photo series built around the classic photo booth feeling — close framing, flash, and the kind of playful energy that happens when people are comfortable at home.

I wanted it to feel a little nostalgic and a little imperfect: the pauses between poses, the half-laughs, and the frames you’d normally keep for yourself.
