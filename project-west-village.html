Side streets, storefront glow, and familiar corners.

A photo series from the West Village — the kind of neighborhood that feels like a movie set even on a random Tuesday. This is about little details: doorways, table tops, street signs, and the way the light hits at the exact right moment.

I wanted the images to feel lived-in — equal parts quiet and social — like stepping outside for “one drink” and ending up with a whole night.
