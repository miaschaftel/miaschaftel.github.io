My name is Mia

I’m a creative designer based in New York City. I’m drawn to how things look, feel, and tell a story, whether that story lives in a digital product, a brand identity, a photograph, or something made by hand.

I studied User Experience Design at the University of Michigan, where I developed a strong foundation in understanding how people interact with products, environments, and ideas. That way of thinking continues to guide my work and extends well beyond screens.

I’ve worked across industries including luxury fashion retail, global hospitality groups, consumer packaged goods, and financial technology, collaborating with teams to shape thoughtful, visually driven experiences. Alongside client work, I spend much of my time exploring creativity through photography, embroidery, improv, jewelry making, painting, writing, and video. These hands-on practices influence how I approach every project, helping me design with intention, curiosity, and a strong point of view.

I’m most energized when I can explore different creative practices to authentically bring ideas to life.
